Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Overview

SignageCMS Player ("the App") is a digital signage client application developed by SignageCMS. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does not:

  • Collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Access your contacts, camera, microphone, or location
  • Share any data with advertising networks or third parties
  • Track your usage or behavior
  • Store credentials on remote servers

Information the App Uses Locally

  • Server URL & Activation Code — stored locally on device to maintain connection to your SignageCMS server
  • Cached Media — images and videos are cached locally on device for offline playback
  • Network Access — the App connects exclusively to the SignageCMS server URL you provide

Permissions Explained

PermissionWhy it is needed
INTERNETConnect to your SignageCMS server to receive content
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE / ACCESS_WIFI_STATEDetect connectivity changes and reconnect automatically
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETEDAuto-start the player after device reboot (kiosk use)
WAKE_LOCKPrevent screen from sleeping during playback
DISABLE_KEYGUARDDisable lock screen for unattended kiosk displays
REQUEST_INSTALL_PACKAGESInstall over-the-air app updates pushed by your SignageCMS admin
WRITE/READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGECache media files for offline playback (Android ≤ 12)

Third-Party Services

The App uses no third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising libraries. The only network communication is with the SignageCMS server URL you configure.

Children's Privacy

This App is a B2B tool intended for businesses and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be noted in the app release notes.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue on GitHub or email support@signagecms.com

مرور کلی

SignageCMS Player یک اپلیکیشن پخش دیجیتال ساینیج است. این سیاست توضیح می‌دهد که اپ به چه اطلاعاتی دسترسی دارد و چگونه از آن‌ها استفاده می‌کند.

اطلاعاتی که جمع‌آوری نمی‌کنیم

  • اطلاعات شخصی (نام، ایمیل، شماره تلفن)
  • دسترسی به مخاطبین، دوربین، میکروفون یا موقعیت مکانی
  • اشتراک‌گذاری داده با شبکه‌های تبلیغاتی یا اشخاص ثالث
  • ردیابی رفتار کاربر

اطلاعاتی که به‌صورت محلی استفاده می‌شود

  • آدرس سرور و کد فعال‌سازی — به‌صورت محلی روی دستگاه ذخیره می‌شود
  • رسانه‌های کَش‌شده — برای پخش آفلاین روی دستگاه کَش می‌شود
  • دسترسی شبکه — اپ فقط با آدرس سرور SignageCMS که شما تعیین می‌کنید ارتباط برقرار می‌کند

نظرة عامة
